What is RiverCity Connect?

RiverCity Connect is a community-focused platform designed to help Louisville residents discover local non-profits, businesses, and support services.

Why It Matters

Access to local resources improves quality of life, strengthens neighborhoods, and ensures support reaches those who need it most.

Get Involved

Know of a resource that should be listed? Help us keep our resources up-to-date by submitting new organizations and services. Together, we can build a stronger, more connected community where everyone has access to the support they need.
